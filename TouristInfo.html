Everything you need to know about your visit to Skopje

Places to see in Skopje

Macedonian Square
Macedonia Square is the main and biggest square of Skopje. It is located in the central part of the city and it crosses the Vardar River. The square is filled with sculptures, bridges, fountains. The main street od Skopje "Macedonia Street" is conected with the square and here you can find a lot of coffe shops, bars and resturants.
The Old Bazzar
The Old Bazaar is a bazaar located in Skopje. It's situated on the eastern bank of the Vardar River.The Old Bazaar is known for its cultural and historical values. Ottoman architecture is predominant. The Old Bazaar is still home to several active mosques, türbes, churches and a clocktower.
Skopje Fortress - Kale
The Skopje Fortress, commonly referred to as Kale is a historic fortress located in the old town of Skopje and situated on the highest point in the city overlooking the Vardar River. The first fortress was built in 6th century AD

Skopje City Museum
Museum of the City of Skopje is a cultural institution. Founded in 1949, it is located in a former railway station that was partly destroyed in the 1963 earthquake.
Mount Vodno
Vodno is a mountain located southwest of the capital city Skopje. The highest point of the mountain is at Krstovar peak, on 1066 meters. The Millennium Cross is a 66-metre tall cross situated on the top of Vodno Mountain. Built in 2002, it is one of the tallest crosses worldwide.
Skopje Aqueduct
The Skopje Aqueduct is an archaeological site located 2 km northwest of central Skopje. The Skopje Aqueduct is the only aqueduct in Macedonia, and one of three largest and well preserved in Yugoslavia.

Lake Kozjak
Lake Kozjak is a paradise for recreationalists, mountaineers, cyclists and visitors who have the opportunity to enjoy clean mountain air and untouched nature. The region is an attractive location because of the beauties of the lake.
Canyon Matka
Matka is a canyon located west of central Skopje.Matka is one of the most popular outdoor destinations and is home to several medieval monasteries. The Matka Lake within the Matka Canyon is the oldest artificial lake in the country.
Church of St.Clement of Ohrid
The Church of Saint Clement of Ohrid often called simply Soborna Crkva is the largest cathedral of the Macedonian Orthodox Church today.
